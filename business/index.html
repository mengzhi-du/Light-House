About Us

Previous Next

At Light House, our philosiphy is to provide our customers with "light for hope". The Light House was first found by Mengzhi and her two co-workers in 2016, in order to offer beautiful and decent lamps to meet various circumstances and needs of our customers. The lamps in the store are either designed by our talented lamp designers or collected from other selective brands. From the classic styles or mordern ones, you can always find a pleasant lamp here. Let the comforts begin with a pleasant light; let the lights light up the day and your heart.





Mengzhi Du

Position:CEO

Graduated from Western University with a Master in Business Administration, Mengzhi found Light House with her great passion on lamps. She holds the belief that a pleasant lamp brings a start of pleasure As the founder of Light House, Mengzhi put much of her effort in order to provide others a comfort environment to all conditions.

Eddie Cole

Position: designer

Eddie Cole became the leader of deginers in Light House in 2016. He specialized in lamps design since 2000. His lamps collections are mainly inspired by industrial modular systems to capture the eohemeral. His great work has been collected and exhibited in different world-known museums and galleries.

Claire Christie

Position: chief operating officer

Christie joint Light House in charge of qualified lamps collection in 2017. With the special experience of 5-year-long sojorum in Europe, Christie had a unique taste on lamps. She and her group keeps exploring more beautiful and exciting lamps designs all over the world, and select them out to meet customers' various needs.
